SQL joins are used to combine rows from two or more tables based on a related column between them. There are several types of SQL joins, including INNER JOIN, LEFT JOIN (or LEFT OUTER JOIN), RIGHT JOIN (or RIGHT OUTER JOIN), and FULL JOIN (or FULL OUTER JOIN). I'll provide examples of each type of join along with tables and queries to illustrate their use.
